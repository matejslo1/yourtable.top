4. FEATURE ROADMAP
-------------------

── FAZA 1 — Product-Market Fit (6–10 tednov) ──────────────────────────────────

Live Floor / Host Mode                                              [Effort: L]
  Kaj: Realtime tloris z barvnimi statusi, drag & drop rezervacije,
       one-click seated/completed/no-show, turn-time alert,
       overbooking warning, walk-in quick seat
  Schema: Aktiviraj Supabase Realtime na reservations + tables

Avtomatski opomniki — SMS + Email                                   [Effort: M]
  Kaj: 24h + 3h opomnik, auto-release nepotrjenih, confirm-with-one-click
  Schema: Implementiraj Twilio SMS. Razširi reminderJob. Dodaj confirmToken.

Deposit / Card Guarantee                                            [Effort: M]
  Kaj: Konfiguracija depozita per slot/day/party size, card hold,
       auto-charge za no-show, partial refund pravila
  Schema: Nov DepositPolicy model. Stripe payment intents (capture later).

Guest Self-Service Link                                             [Effort: S]
  Kaj: Magic link v emailu → guest portal: potrdi, uredi, odpovej
  Schema: Nov GuestToken model (token, reservationId, expiresAt).

Boljši Booking Widget                                               [Effort: M]
  Kaj: Izbira območja, special occasion, white-label CSS, deposit step
  Schema: Razširi widget props. Dodaj area filter na /public/availability.


── FAZA 2 — B2B Value (8–12 tednov) ───────────────────────────────────────────

Guest CRM 2.0                                                       [Effort: M]
  Kaj: VIP nivoji, preference profil, history timeline, auto-tags, LTV score
  Schema: Razširi Guest model: vipLevel, preferences JSON, ltv Decimal.

Analytics Dashboard                                                 [Effort: L]
  Kaj: Revenue per seat, occupancy, heatmap, forecasting, CSV/PDF izvoz
  Schema: Nov /api/v1/analytics. Materialized views.

Waitlist Avtomatizacija                                             [Effort: S]
  Kaj: Auto-offer po prioriteti, SMS offer z accept linkom
  Schema: Razširi waitlistService. Dodaj waitlistOfferJob.

Granular Permissions UI                                             [Effort: M]
  Kaj: UI za permissions po vlogah, audit log viewer, PIN za iPad
  Schema: permissions JSON že obstaja. Dodaj PermissionTemplate.

Review & Follow-up                                                  [Effort: S]
  Kaj: Auto email 2h po COMPLETED, birthday kampanje, "we miss you"
  Schema: Razširi notificationService. Nov reviewFollowupJob.

Shift / Service Period Management                                   [Effort: M]
  Kaj: ServicePeriod (kosilo/večerja) z ločenimi kapacitetami in politikami
  Schema: Nov ServicePeriod model. Razširi OperatingHours.


── FAZA 3 — Diferenciacija (12+ tednov) ────────────────────────────────────────

AI Asistent za Host/Team                                            [Effort: L]
  Kaj: AI seating suggestions, no-show risk score, shift summary, anomaly alerts
  Schema: Claude API integracija. Nov AiInsight model.

Multi-Location / Group Accounts                                     [Effort: XL]
  Kaj: Več lokacij pod enim računom, central reporting, shared guest DB
  Schema: Nov Group model (parent za Tenant).

POS Integracija                                                     [Effort: XL]
  Kaj: Spend sync z rezervacijo, spend per guest, upsell analytics
  Schema: Nov POSTransaction model. Webhook za Square/Lightspeed.

Events / Private Dining                                             [Effort: L]
  Kaj: Event tipi, zaprtje restavracije, prepaid packages
  Schema: Nov Event model z relacijo na Reservation.

Loyalty Program                                                     [Effort: L]
  Kaj: Points/perks, tier rewards, invite-back kampanje
  Schema: Nov LoyaltyAccount, LoyaltyTransaction.
